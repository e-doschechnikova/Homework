The action of the cartoon, presumably, takes place at the end of the 18th century. The prologue tells the story of a young, selfish and spoiled prince who had everything he could want. One winter night, an old beggar comes to the castle and offers the prince a rose in exchange for shelter and salvation from the cold. The haggard look of the old woman causes the prince to feel disgust, and, laughing at her gift, he drives out the beggar. She warns the young man that the appearance is deceiving, and the true beauty is hidden inside, but he laughs at her again. At this moment, the old woman suddenly turns into a beautiful sorceress... As punishment for cruelty, she turns the prince into an ugly monster and casts a powerful spell on the castle with all its inhabitants. Then the Witch leaves the prince with a magic mirror capable of showing events in different parts of the earth, and the very rose that will have to bloom until he turns twenty-one - if he meets a girl and loves her, and can also win her love before the last petal will fall from the rose, the spell will dissipate. Otherwise, the prince will be forever doomed to be a monster.
